Breakfast: Peanut Butter Oatmeal

Ingredients

  • Water 1 cup
  • Oats 1/2 cup
  • Cinnamon 1 tsp
  • Sugar 1 tbsp
  • Honey 2 tbsp
  • Peanut Butter 1/2 cup

Directions

Fill a small pot with 1 cup of purified water and put it to boil. While you wait put the oats, cinnamon, sugar, honey, and peanut butter in a bowl together. Leave the peanut butter on top stuck on the spoon. Once the water reaches a boil pour it slowly over the side of the spoon stuck with peanut butter until the spoon is clean and the oats are floating. Let the oats soak and the peanut butter melt for a minute then mix it all together. Eat it before it gets too cold.
