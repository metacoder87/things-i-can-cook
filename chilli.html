THINGS I CAN COOK

Dinner: Chilli

Ingredients

  • Joan of Ark Dark red kidney beans 1 can
  • Joan of Ark Light red kidney beans 1 can
  • Brooks Chilli beans 1 big can
  • Campbells Tomato Juice 1 big can
  • Chipotle Chilli Pepper 1 tsp
  • Cayene Pepper 1 tsp
  • Chilli Powder 1/8 cup
  • Cooker 4 qt
  • Ground Beef 2 lbs lean
  • Onion 1 whole
  • Salt & Pepper

Directions

  1. Boil all of the beans in the cooker with seasoning.
  2. Brown the beef with onion finely minced and salt and pepper.
  3. Mix together and cook until thick.
  4. Add mozzarella cheese and ritz crackers as wanted.
