Dinner: Beef Stew

Ingredients

  • Steak 2 lbs
  • Carrots 1 lbs
  • Celery 3 stalks
  • Onion 1 purple
  • Garlic 3 cloves
  • Potatoes 4 medium
  • Butter 4 oz
  • Olive Oil 4 oz
  • Salt 1 tsp
  • Pepper 1 tsp

Directions

  1. Beat the steak and cut into cubes
  2. Boil 2 qts of water in a 4 qt pot.
  3. Add the steak to the boiling water.
  4. Boil on medium for 30 minutes.
  5. Prepare vegetables
  6. Add the garlic.
  7. Boil for 30 minutes.

    8. Add water as needed to keep from burning.

  8. Add onion to the pot.
  9. Boil for 30 minutes.
  10. Add the potatoes to the pot.
  11. Boil for 1 hour.
  12. Add the celery into the pot.

    13. Keep adding water but ensure it isn't too soupy.

  13. Boil for 1 hour.
  14. Add the carrots into the pot.
  15. Boil for 30 minutes.

    16. Enjoy...
