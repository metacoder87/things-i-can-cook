THINGS I CAN COOK

Desserts: Cowdabs

Ingredients

  • Oats 4 cups
  • Sugar 2 cups
  • Butter 4 oz
  • Peanut Butter 1 cup
  • Carnation Evaporated Milk 1/2 cup
  • Cocoa 3 tbsp
  • Vanilla 1 tsp
  • Cooker 4 qts
  • Wax Paper 1 roll

Directions

  1. Mix sugar and cocoa together in cooker.
  2. Turn on medium heat.
  3. Stir constantly until finished.
  4. Add the half stick of butter.
  5. Stir until melted and chocolate is ready.
  6. Add the evaporated milk.
  7. Stir until it starts to bubble a little.
  8. Mix in the peanut butter.
  9. Add the vanilla.
  10. Mix in all of the oats.
  11. As soon as all is mixed scoop.
  12. Leave in spoon sized piles on wax paper.
  13. Once cool enjoy with milk.
