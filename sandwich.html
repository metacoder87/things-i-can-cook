THINGS I CAN COOK

Lunch: Sandwich

Ingredients

  • Bread 2 slices
  • Miracle Whip 1 jar
  • Deli Meat 3 slices
  • Cheese 2 slices
  • Lettuce
  • Garlic 1 clove
  • Onion 1 slice
  • Salt 1 shake
  • Pepper 2 shakes
  • Olive Oil 1 dab

Directions

  1. Spread the Miracle Whip on one slice of bread.
  2. Put the cheese on that slice of bread.
  3. Add the meat with the edges hanging over.
  4. Add the onion and garlic to the meat and fold the meat over.
  5. Add the salt and pepper.
  6. Add the lettuce.
  7. Add the dab of olive oil to the lettuce.
  8. Add the top slice of bread to complete the sandwich.

    9. Enjoy with a cold beer...
