Dessert: Peanut Butter Cookies

Ingredients

  • Peanut Butter 1 cup
  • Sugar 1 cup
  • Egg 1 large
  • Vanilla 1 tsp

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 325 degrees.
  2. Mix ingredients together.
  3. Roll into cookie size balls.
  4. Place on non-stick cookie sheet.
  5. Cover the bottom of a bowl with sugar.
  6. Take a wet fork and stick it in the sugar.
  7. Smash the cookie balls with the fork.
  8. Make a cross shape on the top.
  9. Keep sugar on the fork.
  10. Bake for 15 minutes.

    11. Enjoy with a glass of cold milk...
