THINGS I CAN COOK

Desserts: Fudge

Ingredients

  • Sugar 2 cups
  • Butter 4 tbsp
  • Milk 1 cup
  • Cocoa 3 tbsp
  • Vanilla 1 tsp

Directions

  1. Grease an 8x8 inch square baking pan. Set aside.
  2. Combine sugar, cocoa and milk in a medium saucepan.
  3. Stir to blend, then bring to a boil, stirring constantly.
  4. Reduce heat and simmer. Do not stir again.
  5. Place candy thermometer in pan and cook until temperature reaches 238 degrees F.
  6. Cook until a drop of this mixture in a cup of cold water forms a soft ball.
  7. Remove from heat.
  8. Add butter or margarine and vanilla extract.
  9. Beat with a wooden spoon until the fudge loses its sheen. Do not under beat.
  10. Pour into prepared pan and let cool.
  11. Cut into about 60 squares.
  12. Once cool enjoy with milk.
