Lunch: Peanut Butter & Jelly Sandwich

Ingredients

  • Peanut Butter 1 jar
  • Jelly 1 jar
  • Bread 2 slices
  • Milk 1 glass
  • Butter Knife 2
  • Plate 1

Directions

  1. Put the bread on the plate.
  2. Scoop a lot of peanut butter with the knife.
  3. Spread peanut butter on one slice of bread.
  4. Scoop a little less jelly than peanut butter with the other knife.
  5. Spread the jelly on the other slice of bread.
  6. Put the slices of bread together.
  7. Cut in half and/or remove crust if you like.
  8. Enjoy with milk.
