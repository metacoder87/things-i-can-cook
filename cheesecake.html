THINGS I CAN COOK

Dessert: Cheesecake

Ingredients

  • Cream Cheese 8 oz
  • Sugar 1/3 cup
  • Cool Whip 6 oz
  • Peanut Butter 1/3 cup
  • Graham Cracker Crumbs 1.25 cups
  • Honey 1 tbsp
  • Butter 1 tbsp
  • Pie Pan 1

Directions

  1. Crush approximately one package of graham crackers.
  2. Add margarine and honey and blend with a fork until smooth.
  3. Spread butter or vegetable oil on 9-inch pie pan.
  4. Press crumbs into pan.
  5. Bake at 350 for 10 minutes or until edges feel firm and dry.

    6. Let cool while...

  6. In a large bowl beat together the cream cheese, peanut butter and sugar until smooth.
  7. Gently fold in the whipped cream.
  8. Spoon into the prepared crust.
  9. Refrigerate 3 hours, or until set.
