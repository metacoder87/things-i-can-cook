Desserts: Brownies

Ingredients

  • Butter melted 1 cup
  • Sugar 2 cups
  • Cocoa 1/2 cup
  • Eggs 4 large
  • Flour All-Purpose 1 & 1/2 cups
  • Vanilla 1 tsp
  • Salt 1/2 tsp

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Grease 9x13-inch pan.
  3. Mix everything together.
  4. Spread the batter into the pan.
  5. Bake for 20-30 minutes.
  6. Bake until a tooth pick can be inserted and not be wet.
  7. Let the brownies cool.
  8. Once cool enjoy with milk.
