THINGS I CAN COOK

Breakfast: Omlet

Ingredients

  • Eggs 3 large
  • Diced Meat 1/4 cup
  • Shredded Cheese 1/4 cup
  • Butter 3 tbsp
  • Salt & Pepper

Directions

  1. Brown the diced meat if not already cooked.
  2. Crack the eggs into a bowl and fold until a smooth orange.
  3. Melt butter in a frying pan.
  4. Pour eggs into frying pan.
  5. Add meat(cooked), cheese, salt & pepper.
  6. Fry until fluffy and golden brown.
