Lunch: Grilled Cheese

Ingredients

  • Bread 2 slices
  • American Cheese 2 slices
  • Butter 2 oz

Directions

  1. Melt 1 oz of butter in the pan.
  2. Put the cheese between the bread.
  3. Put the sandwich in the pan.
  4. Fry one side until butter is gone.
  5. Flip the sandwich to the other side.
  6. Add the rest of the butter.
  7. Cook until nice and crispy on both sides.
  8. Watch out cheese inside is hot.
