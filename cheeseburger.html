Dinner: Cheeseburger

Ingredients

  • Hamburger bun 1
  • Hamburger 8 oz
  • American Cheese 2 slices
  • Bacon 2 strips
  • Lettuce 2 leaves
  • Onion 1 slice
  • Garlic 1 clove
  • Miracle Whip 1 jar
  • Kethcup 1 bottle

Directions

  1. Fine chop the garlic.
  2. Mix the garlic and the beef.
  3. Flatten beef into a patty.
  4. Fry until well done and melt the cheese.
  5. Toast the bun.
  6. Spread the Miracle Whip.
  7. Add the beef and cheese.
  8. Add the bacon, lettuce and onion.
  9. Add the ketchup.
  10. Serve with cold beer.
